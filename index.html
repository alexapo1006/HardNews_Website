GTA 5 postponed for the March 25th

After initially, Rockstar announced the release of the new Grand Theft Auto PC on January 27th, they postponed the event until March 25th. But that notice stated the official minimum specs to run the game.It requires minimum quad-core processor, 4GB of RAM, 1GB video memory and an amount of 65GB free space, which is really, a lot.

Spiral 3, the next Google's Project Ara Phone

The future modular phone from Google sounds promising.At The Ara Developers Conference, the head of the project, Paul Eremenko stated that for this model are available from 20 to 30 components (modules) and supplied battery has a range of 24 hours, specifies Engadget.The innovation of Spiral 3 Ara consists in moving the electromagnets that supports the modules in the phone endoskeleton.

Mail Pilot 2 has been announced!

Mail Pilot is an email application based on gestures and reminders, making from Inbox a To-Do list. Similar to Mailbox, the application being bought by Dropbox is a more refined and more developed version, which has companion for Mac,too.
